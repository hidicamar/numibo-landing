1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files a website stores in your browser. We use them to make the application work and, only with your consent, for analytics on public pages.

2. Essential cookies

These cookies are required for the application to function and are always set; without them, signing in and using the application is not possible.

  • Session cookie – keeps you signed in and preserves your settings during a visit; expires after at most 2 hours of inactivity.
  • XSRF-TOKEN – protects forms against abuse (CSRF protection).
  • "Remember me" cookie – set only if you choose to stay signed in.

3. Analytics cookies (Google Analytics)

On our public pages we use Google Analytics to understand site traffic. These cookies are set only with your consent given through the cookie banner. We do not use analytics cookies inside the logged-in area of the application.

  • _ga, _ga_* – distinguishing visitors and measuring traffic; valid for up to 2 years.

4. Managing cookies

You can withdraw your consent to analytics cookies at any time in the cookie settings on the website. You can also delete or block cookies in your browser settings; blocking essential cookies may prevent the application from working. See our privacy policy for details on data processing.
